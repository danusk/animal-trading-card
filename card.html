Emperor Penguin

Emperor penguins can dive up to 530 meters for as long as 20 minutes in water, the deepest and longest dive of any bird.

  • Scientific Name: Aptenodytes forsteri
  • Average Height: 115 centimeters
  • Average Life Span: 20 years
  • Habitat: Antarctic

Emperor penguins live in Antarctica, facing temperatures as low as -60°C. Besides their layers of insulating feathers and fat that protect them from the cold, they also huddle close together in groups to keep warm.
